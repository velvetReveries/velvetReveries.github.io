Server Rules

Please read and follow these rules to ensure a positive experience for everyone

General Rules

Power-gaming

Power-gaming is strictly forbidden. This refers to playing with the aim of maximizing personal progress at the expense of storytelling, atmosphere, and camaraderie. It's considered unsporting and can undermine the roleplaying experience for others.

Server Etiquette and Immersion

Glyph RP aims to create a unique and immersive fantasy role-play server. Breaking immersion, not respecting the story, or disregarding character freedom and individuality is discouraged. The community values atmosphere and character engagement above all.

Respect for Others

Players are expected to be respectful, sociable, and fun to interact with. Unsociable behavior, argumentativeness, or toxic engagement is not tolerated, and may result in moderation or banning.

Anti-Cheat, Fair Play & No Exploiting

Cheating, exploiting bugs, hacking, or using unauthorized mods is forbidden. Actions that give unfair advantage or disrupt fair play are against server rules.

Roleplay Integrity & Lore

Focused storytelling and character development are encouraged. Players should not metagame or use out-of-character knowledge to influence in-game decisions.

Custom Server Features

The server features custom creature spawns, starting questlines, reputation systems, guilds, and magicks. Respect and participate in these systems as intended by the server design.

Server Processes & Technical Rules

Server restarts happen daily at 5:30am and 1:30pm EST. Players must save progress and log out properly during these periods.

Discord Rules

Communication Standards

Maintain respectful communication in all Discord channels. Follow channel-specific guidelines and use appropriate channels for different topics.

Voice Chat Etiquette

Use push-to-talk when possible, minimize background noise, and respect others' conversations in voice channels.

Moderation

Follow moderator instructions promptly. Disputes should be handled privately with staff members.

Character Creation

Character Guidelines

Create characters that fit within the server's lore and setting. Ensure your character has depth and reasonable limitations.

Character Backgrounds

Develop meaningful backstories that enhance roleplay opportunities and fit within the established world.

Combat & PvP Rules

Fair Combat

Engage in combat with consideration for roleplay and story. Avoid power-gaming or taking unfair advantages.

Consent and Consequences

Respect roleplay consequences and obtain appropriate consent for significant character actions.

Building Rules

Construction Guidelines

Build structures that are lore-appropriate and considerate of the server's aesthetic and other players' experiences.

Territory and Resources

Respect established territories and resource locations. Coordinate with other players when building in shared areas.
