What is Glyph RP all about?

Glyph RP aims to create the most immersive and unique fantasy role-play servers that focuses on engaging content, character freedom and individuality. From the very first time you load into our games, you’ll experience a unique starting experience different from all others. From there you will begin engaging in our content and developing your character. We even employ a patch-based content system to keep the game fresh during each season!
